Preston-Werner Ventures Rolling Fund

Led by Tom Preston-Werner (GitHub cofounder), this fund invests in early-stage technology companies. Our three-partner team, consisting of active founder-operators, also provides hands-on assistance, peer-to-peer support, and VC networking to increase the likelihood of companies' success. We actively participate in follow-on rounds, building on Tom's successful track record as an angel investor the past decade.

We created the fund in early 2023 using the AngelList Rolling Fund vehicle. We fundraise publicly and continuously. As of October 1st, 2023, we have completed 46 investments.
