In order to foster more climate dialogue and community, we gather with friends monthly to share our knowledge and learn through discussion. So far, we've covered:

  • Climate Science
  • Sequestration & Negative Emissions
  • Energy Sources & Electrification
  • Domestic Climate Policy
  • Environmental Justice
  • Climate Philanthropy

We hope to open these discussions to a wider audience as we develop the program.
